What this site is

Part portfolio, part lab notebook, part place to dump things I don’t want to forget.

I work across IT operations and security: endpoint management, access, onboarding, and the kind of glue work that keeps everything from falling apart quietly in the background.

On the security side, I spend time in labs practicing enumeration, exploitation, and privilege escalation in controlled environments. The goal is to build instincts that carry over into real systems, not just collect tool names.

This site is where I collect writeups, small projects, and notes from that work. Some of it is polished, some of it is just “this worked, here’s why.”
